About the Inaugurals

The Pakistan Entrepreneurial Society brings together a collection of doers, thinkers, innovators, explorers, visionaries, teachers, learners and seeks to illuminate, inspire, change perceptions, incite action and foster new connections.

We are focused on proffering a stage to the best young entrepreneurs of Pakistan and an experience for inspirational and intellectual acceleration. Our event strategically allocates a window for collaboration of communication, economics and entrepreneurship - ideas that are close to our central theme: Pioneering Progress.

About The Pakistan Entrepreneurial Society


In the spirit of pioneering progress, The Pakistan Entrepreneurial Society is a an organisation for local, high school students that will represent Pakistan at the international stage of entrepreneurs. The Inaugurals will be held every year to select a this team of 10 members 2 of which will be reserve. At the moment they will proceed to participate in the Entrepreneurial World Cup and The Global Youth Entrepreneurship Challenge.
